About the Conference

1st International Conference on Advances in Big Data, Cloud, and Intelligent Computing (ICABCIC-2025) was successfully organized in collaboration with leading institutions and industry experts. The conference provided a global platform for researchers, academicians, and professionals to share their latest innovations in Big Data, Cloud Computing, and Artificial Intelligence. With over 10 distinguished speakers and more than 50 research papers presented across 5 technical sessions, ICABCIC-2025 highlighted the significance of emerging technologies in shaping the future of intelligent computing.

The conference fostered collaboration among researchers, industry leaders, and technology enthusiasts, encouraging discussions on cutting-edge solutions and future trends. Attendees had the opportunity to present their research, engage in insightful discussions, and explore groundbreaking advancements in data science, cloud architecture, and AI-driven solutions. With a diverse range of topics and interactive sessions, ICABCIC-2025 played a crucial role in shaping the next generation of intelligent computing and digital transformation.
