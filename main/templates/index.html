About the Conference

1st International Conference on Multidisciplinary Research in Education, Science, and Technology (ICMRST-2025) was successfully organized on December 29th & 30th, 2025, in Goa. The conference was held in collaboration with three renowned universities: Tunghai University, Swaminarayan University, and Vadodara Institute-Kotambi. ICMRST-2025 provided a platform for global scholars and researchers to exchange innovative research and discuss emerging trends in education, science, and technology. With over 12 reputed speakers and more than 40 research papers presented across 4 technical sessions, the conference highlighted the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration and advancing research in various fields.

The conference aimed to foster collaboration between educators, researchers, and practitioners across diverse disciplines, encouraging meaningful dialogue and the sharing of groundbreaking ideas. Attendees had the opportunity to present their research, gain insights from leading experts, and explore new strategies for addressing the challenges faced by the education and technology sectors. With its diverse range of topics and interactive sessions, ICMRST-2025 played a key role in shaping the future of education, science, and technology research.
